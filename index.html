Dinesh Mathaniya

Founder & CEO

Dinesh Mathaniya is the visionary Founder & CEO of Mathaniya Technologies, a premier IT solutions provider based in Jodhpur, Rajasthan. With over a decade of experience in the ever-evolving digital landscape, Dinesh has established himself as a thought leader in Website Development, Digital Marketing, and Business Automation.

His journey began with a simple yet profound mission: to bridge the technological gap for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in India. Dinesh recognized early on that while technology was advancing rapidly, many local businesses were struggling to adapt and leverage these tools for growth. Driven by a passion for innovation and a deep desire to see fellow entrepreneurs succeed, he founded Mathaniya Technologies.

Under his dynamic leadership, the company has grown from a modest startup into a full-fledged digital agency, serving over 500 satisfied clients across various industries. Dinesh's expertise lies not just in coding or marketing strategies, but in understanding the core pulse of a business. He specializes in creating custom digital identities that resonate with target audiences, implementing automation tools that save hundreds of man-hours, and crafting marketing campaigns that deliver tangible ROI.

Dinesh is a strong advocate for 'Digital India' and actively conducts workshops and seminars to educate business owners about the power of the internet. He believes that a website is not just a digital address, but a 24/7 salesperson that never sleeps. His approach combines technical precision with creative flair, ensuring that every project delivered by Mathaniya Technologies is not only functional but also aesthetically stunning.
